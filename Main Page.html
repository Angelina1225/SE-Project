About Us

We at IIT Jodhpur, provide staff and students the best quality food, which is healthy and hygienic. We have all the needed equipment and are self-sufficient to prepare meals accordingly. We aim to provide balanced nutrition to consumers at competitive prices.

A scheduled menu is provided by the IIT Jodhpur Canteen service to offer a variety of food items for the students and staff to choose from. The menu includes the following: pasta, Sandwiches, Desserts, Juices and Milk. Our menu items are hand-tailored by our dietitians, consisting of major portions of healthy lean meats, fruits, and vegetables. We offer food from around the globe. This helps students to learn and taste food from different regions of the world.
IIT Jodhpur Canteen is open for all, from 12 PM to 2AM everyday.
